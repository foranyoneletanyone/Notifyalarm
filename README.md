NotifyAlarm

NotifyAlarm is an Android app that monitors incoming notifications and triggers a loud customizable alarm when specific keywords are detected.
It is designed for people who do not want to miss important notifications while gaming, studying, sleeping, or working.

Features
🔔 Detects keywords inside notifications
🎵 Custom alarm sounds
🧠 Supports multiple alarms at the same time
⚙️ AND / OR keyword operators
🔡 Optional case-sensitive matching
📱 Works with notifications from any app
⏰ Instant alert system
🛠 Lightweight and easy to configure
How It Works

NotifyAlarm listens to incoming notifications on your device.

When a notification contains the configured keyword(s), the app automatically plays the selected alarm sound.

Example Rules
OR Operator

Trigger alarm if notification contains:

urgent
important

Result:

"urgent meeting" ✅
"important update" ✅
AND Operator

Trigger alarm only if notification contains:

boss
call

Result:

"boss is calling" ✅
"call me later" ❌
Permissions

NotifyAlarm requires Notification Access permission to read incoming notifications.

The app does not store or upload your notifications.

Configuration

Each alarm rule supports:

Setting	Description
Keywords	Words to monitor
Operator	AND / OR
Case Sensitive	Enable exact matching
Alarm Sound	Choose custom sound
Enabled	Turn rule on/off
Privacy

NotifyAlarm processes notifications locally on your device.

No notification data is collected, stored, or sent to external servers.
